Learn about IEEE

IEEE is the world’s largest technical
professional organization dedicated to
advancing technology for the benefit of
humanity.

IEEE and its members inspire a global community to innovate for a better tomorrow through its more than 423,000
members in over 160 countries, and its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional
and educational activities. IEEE is the trusted “voice” for engineering, computing, and technology information around the
globe.
